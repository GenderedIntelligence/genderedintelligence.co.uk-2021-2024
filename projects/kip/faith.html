Christianity

Support Groups and Other Organisations

  • MCC (Metropolitan Community Church of North London)

LGBT-welcoming spiritual community with a weekly service in Camden, London.

  • LGBT+ Catholics Westminster

We're a group of LGBT+ Catholics attached to the Church of the Immaculate Conception at Farm Street, who meet for Mass and socialising every second Sunday of the month. I was wondering whether we might be included in your list of faith support groups? While we're part of the the RC Archdiocese of Westminster's pastoral provision to LGBT+ Catholics, their families and friends, we are not associated with groups like Courage. You may have also heard of us as the group that met for the 'Soho Masses', which was founded after the bombing of the Admiral Duncan pub - we moved to Farm Street in 2013.

Islam

Online resources

  • Queer Muslim master post at IAmNotHaram's tumblr

Links to LGBT and LGBT-friendly Muslim blogs, books, answers to concern like “I need proof from Qur’an and Sunnah that I’m not Haraam”, plus tips for dealing with a mental health crisis.

  • American organisation HRC has written a booklet for LGBT Muslims about reconciling their faith with being LGBT: Coming Home to Islam and to Self.

  • The American organisation Muslims for Progressive Values also lists resources for LGBTQI Muslims.

  • Hidayah UK - led by and for LGBTQ+ people, Hidayah (meaning guidance in Arabic) helps provide support for LGBTQIA+ Muslims and promotes social justice and education about the community to counter discrimination, prejudice and injustice. See their event calendar for upcoming events

Support Groups and other Organisations

  • In London there is a new transgender Muslim support Group called Intersections that takes place once per month at 56 Dean Street in Soho.

  • There are an increasing number of mosques and other Islamic community spaces that accept all people, whatever their gender identity including Inclusive Mosque Initiative

  • Imaan LGBT is a support group for LGBT Muslims in the UK, with groups in London, Birmingham and Manchester.
