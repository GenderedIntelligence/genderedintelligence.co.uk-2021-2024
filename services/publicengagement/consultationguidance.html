Consultation Guidance

Last year the government published guidance for schools on how to support transgender students.

The guidance was first promised in 2018, but repeated delays and conflicts within the government left educators waiting over five years.

The guidance is non-statutory, meaning it does not change the law, but it is still important and useful to respond. It is open to public consultation until March 12th 2024. You can respond to the consultation here.

We agree with some of the fundamental principles of this guidance. However, we are concerned about the distressing language used towards trans pupils and their friends and families, and about the proposed exclusion of trans pupils from toilets, sports, and physical activity.
